Highlights

Rich in archaeological and numismatic collections, the museum has the largest collection of Roman antiquities, outside Europe

Visitors get to see the country's best collection of South Indian bronzes, both ancient and modern icons of Nataraja, Rama, Sita, Lakshmana and Hanuman. The museum has mainly six buildings.
